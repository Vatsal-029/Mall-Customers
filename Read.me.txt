Problem Statement:
Identify customers who spend more money buying things in mall.
Identify each customer's spending category:
1. Careless spender
2. Standard customer
3. Target customer
4. Sensible spender
5. Careful spender
Also tell the business which customers spend more than others (target customers)

Column names : Description
1. CustomerID : Customer ID
2. Gender : Male or Female
3. Annual_Income(k$) : Annual income in thousand ($)
4. Spending_Score(1-100) : Spending score of each customer range (1-100)
